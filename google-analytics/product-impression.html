Product impression

Event name view_item_list
Event type GA4 recommended
Event trigger A customer views a list of product cards on any product listing page in your online store.

Event parameters

This event is based on the Google Analytics 4 (GA4) specifications and includes the following parameters. The following table uses dot notation.

Parameter Value
event view_item_list
ecommerce The record of items viewed by the customer.
.currency The currency code for the prices of products listed in the event.
.items A list of specific items viewed by the customer.
..item_id The retailerProductID of the product.
..item_name The name of the product.
..index The position (0,1,2,3,4…) of the product on the page on which the product is displayed to the customer.
..item_brand The brand of the product.
..item_category The level 1 product category the product belongs to.
..item_category2 The level 2 product category the product belongs to.
..item_category3 The level 3 product category the product belongs to.
..item_category4 The level 4 product category the product belongs to.
..item_category5 The level 5 product category the product belongs to.
..item_category_more The subsequent product categories (levels 6, 7, 8…) the product belongs to.
..hfss_display_restriction_group The HFSS (high in fat, sugar, and salt) display restriction group associated with the product.
..item_list_name The page type and name of the section where the product is displayed, in page type:section name format.

For example, for a product in the Other section on the Product details page, the list parameter value is displayed as product-details: other.
..item_variant The variant of the product.
..price The price of the product.
..rating The overall rating submitted by customers for the product.
..quantity The quantity of the product. By default, this is set to 1.
..is_on_promotion The boolean value (true or false) to indicate whether the product is on promotion.
..promotion_ids Comma-separated list of active retailerPromotionIds for this product.
..is_favorite The boolean value (true or false) to indicate whether the product is a customer’s favorite.
..is_featured The boolean value (true or false) to indicate whether the product is associated with a featured products campaign.
..featured_campaign_id The ID of the featured products campaign the product is associated with.
..featured_campaign_name The name of the featured products campaign the product is associated with.
..can_add_to_cart The boolean value (true or false) to indicate whether the customer can add this product to their cart.
