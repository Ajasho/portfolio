Raw produce segregation - NEW (EC-317)

Ecommerce | CFC | Fulfillment Management | Business

Release date: January 31, 2025

Details

OSP now lets your customers choose whether they want their raw produce items, such as fresh fruits, meat, or fish to be bagged separately from other product types.

Customers can select their preference in a new section called Delivery preferences on the Account settings page in your online store.

The fulfillment teams will adhere to this preference when preparing the order.

Separating raw produce items from other products can help maintain food safety, prevent cross-contamination, and ensure the quality and freshness of the produce during transportation and delivery.

This feature can also help you comply with legal requirements, if applicable in your operating region.

Raw produce segregation is only supported for orders picked in an OSP CFC.

Enablement

This feature is available now for OSP web and mobile apps from the following version numbers:

  • iOS 1.360
  • Android 0.390.0

This feature is optional. Speak to your Ocado contact to enable it for you.

Further information

For updated documentation, see:

  • Online store - Account settings

For further information about this change, please speak to your Ocado contact.

Reason for change

This release implements the OSP roadmap feature EC-317.
