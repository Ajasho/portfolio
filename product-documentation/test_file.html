Online store - Product cards

Learn what product cards are and the different options to configure them.

Product cards are visual representation of products that you can display in your online store. Using product cards, you can convey important information and features of the product, like its name, image, price, and availability. You can also use product cards to display additional details like customer ratings.

By providing customers with their first impression of a product they are viewing, product cards help customers to decide if a product is suitable for purchase, requires further consideration, or unsuitable for their needs.

Product card layouts

Your OSP displays product cards in a grid-like layout on all of your product listing pages, which lets your customers quickly browse and compare different products.

Depending on the platform your online store operates in, product cards can be displayed in two different modes:

  • Portrait

  • Landscape
