Online store - Product details pages

Learn what product details pages are and the various information you can display on them.

Product details pages are dedicated pages to provide additional information about products in your online store.

Use these pages to provide customers with the information they need to know before making a purchase, for example, product description, price, storage information, or nutritional data.

You can also use these pages to display customer ratings and reviews.

Access the product details pages

Customers can access a product details page by clicking a product card on any of the product listing pages.

Components

You can use the product details page to display the following information about the product:

Component Example/ Description
Product images

High-quality images of the product. Customers can also zoom in on the images to get a closer look at the product before making a purchase decision.

To display a product image:

1. Upload the image by using the Product Images resource with imageType field set to PRODUCT.
2. Link the image to the product by using the retailerProductImageIds in the Product Propositions resource.

You can also blur the images of certain products, like alcohol, to restrict their visibility in your online store. See the Selling restricted products explainer for more information.
Product title

The name or title of the product. This is the value you set in the description field in the Product Propositions resource.
Bottle deposit

A refundable fee charged on certain beverage containers, like plastic or glass bottles, that the customer can return for recycling.

To display the bottle deposit:

1. Create and configure the bottle deposit using the Product Deposits resource
2. Link the bottle deposit to the product using the deposits field in the Product Propositions resource.
Additional Product Charges (APC)

The additional product charges that will be applied to the product price.

You can configure the APC for a product using the Additional product charges resource.
Guaranteed product life / Shelf life

The information about the guaranteed shelf life of the product.

This is the value you set in the guaranteedProductLifeInDays field in the Product Propositions resource.
Pack size

The quantity or number of items contained in a single package or unit. For example, 200g, 1L. The pack size informs the customer about the quantity of the product they are purchasing.

This is the value you set in the pack object in the Product Storage resource.
Country of origin

The product’s country of origin, displayed prominently above the product price, in the same font size and color as that of the product price, to comply with South Korean regulations. This only applies to online stores in South Korea.

This is the value you set in the countryOfOrigin object in the Product Propositions resource.
Product price

The final price of the product after all the discounts have been applied and the deductions have been subtracted from its original or gross price. This excludes any additional costs or fees that the customer may need to pay.

You can set the price of a product using the Product Price resource.
Strikethrough original price

The original price of the product striked through. This is a formatting convention to indicate a discounted or sale price.

The strikethrough original price is displayed if there is an applicable discount that would lower the price of the product for a given customer.
Unit price/ Price-per

The price of a single item or a standardized measure of the product, for example, £1 each, £2.00 per 100g.

You can set the price-per value for a product using the Product Price resource.
Gross price

The total price of the product before any deductions, promotions, or discounts are applied. It includes all costs associated with the product, such as taxes or additional fees.
Catch-weight details

Indicates the price of products that may vary in weight. The final price is determined by the actual weight at the time of purchase.

This is the value you set in the catchweightDetails object in the Product Storage resource.

For more information, see the Catch-weight products explainer.
Add button

Adds the product to the customer’s cart.

You can also configure the Add button to remain fixed at the top of the product details page as the customer scrolls past the original button. This ensures that customers can easily add the product to their cart without needing to scroll back up. To enable the sticky Add button, speak to your Ocado contact.
Promotions

The different promotions associated with the product.

Clicking a promotion tile redirects the customer to the respective promotion details page. For more information, see the Promotions explainer.
Coupons

The coupon promotions associated with the product. Clicking the coupon tile redirects the customer to the respective coupon details page.

You must have the Coupons feature enabled in your OSP to create and configure coupon promotions in your online store. For more information, see the Coupons explainer.
Last bought date

The date the product was last purchased by the customer.
Lifestyle icons

Icons that you can display to categorize the product according to a dietary or lifestyle choice. You can use the icons to highlight the major selling points, or provide any additional information about the product, for example, vegan, gluten-free, or microwavable.

For more information, see the Dietary and lifestyle icons section in the Product filters explainer.
Add to Favourites button

Adds the product to the customer’s Favorites. For more information, see the Favorites explainer.
Add to List button

Adds the product to a customer’s shopping list. This button is displayed only if you have the Shopping lists feature enabled in your online store.

For more information, see the Shopping lists explainer.
Add to Regulars button

Adds the product to the customer’s Regulars. This button is displayed only if you have the Regulars feature enabled in your online store.

For more information, see the Regulars explainer.
Share button

Allows the customer to share the product details page. The share functionality is only available on the mobile apps version of your online store.
Nutri-Score label

A 5-letter, 5-color label used to demonstrate the overall nutritional value of food products and comply with local legal requirements.

For more information, see the Nutri-Score labels explainer.
Detailed product images

Images of the product that you can display above the Detailed information section in addition to the main product images to convey more detailed information about the product.

To display a detailed product image:

1. Upload the image by using the Product Images resource with imageType set to DETAIL.
2. Link the image to the product by using the retailerDetailedImageIds in the Product Propositions resource.

Customers can click See more to see all the detailed images associated with the product.
Detailed information

The additional and extended details about the product, presented under different sections like Product information, Dietary information, and Nutritional data.

This is the value you set in the detailedInformation field in the Product Propositions resource.
Related products

Products recommended by OSP that have a high probability of being purchased along with the product being viewed by the customer. For example, for the customer who’s viewing apples, this section will display oranges, if customers who buy apples also tend to buy oranges in your online store.

These recommendations are displayed only if you have the Contextual product recommendations feature enabled in your online store. For more information, see the Contextual product recommendations explainer.

If you manage the content in your online store using the Contentful web app, you can configure the featured products to be displayed in the Related products section. For more information, see the Featured products user guide.
Customer ratings

The overall ratings submitted by customers for a product. Customers can access the Overall ratings and Customer reviews sections by clicking on the product ratings.

Customer ratings are displayed only if you have the Product reviews feature enabled in your online store. For more information, see the Product reviews explainer.
Overall ratings

The aggregated scores given by customers to the product, displayed as stars, for example, 4 out of 5 stars, with a breakdown of all ratings. These ratings provide a quick summary of customer satisfaction.

The overall ratings are displayed only if you have the Product reviews feature enabled in your online store. For more information, see the Product reviews explainer.
Add a review button

Opens a form that lets customers submit a rating or a review about the product.

The button is displayed only if you have the Product reviews feature enabled in your online store. For more information, see the Product reviews explainer.
Customer reviews

The reviews or feedback submitted by customers about the product. These reviews can offer valuable insights about the product to potential customers, helping them make informed decisions before buying the product.

The customer reviews are displayed only if you have the Product reviews feature enabled in your online store. For more information, see the Product reviews explainer.
