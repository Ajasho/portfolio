Coupons

Learn how OSP lets you create coupons for customers in your online store.

Coupons are a type of promotion which can target specific customer IDs in your online store. They are simple price discounts that are automatically applied when a customer adds a product with a coupon on it to their cart.

Additionally, OSP also provides a dedicated Coupons page to showcase all the coupons a specific customer is eligible for.

Instead of providing generic discounts to all customers, you can use coupons to provide your customers, a more personalized and engaging shopping experience. If targeted to the right customers, coupons can unlock opportunities to grow sales and improve customer loyalty.

Currently, OSP only supports coupons as a simple promotion on a single list of products with a required quantity to buy.

Key benefits

With the coupons feature, you can:

  • Create personalized promotions by targeting specific customers. This can increase customer engagement and loyalty, and grow sales.
  • Manage customer price perception of new and existing brands and products.
  • Manage inventory by promoting new or slow-moving products, or clearing out excess stock.
  • Generate additional revenue from suppliers by showcasing their products or brands on the Coupons page.

Limitations

Currently, OSP only lets you assign coupons to specific products, promotion product groups, customer IDs, and customer group IDs. You cannot apply coupons to customer segments.

To learn more about the difference between customer groups and customer segments, see the Customer Segments and Customer Groups section in the Customer segments explainer.

Enablement

The Coupons feature is an optional feature available for both the web and mobile apps versions of your OSP online store.

To create coupons in OSP, the Coupons page must be enabled in your online store. You must also enable the Coupon configuration section in the Promotions web app if you use the Promotions web app to create promotions.

Speak to your Ocado contact for more information.

Coupons page

The Coupons page is the main space to display all the active coupon promotions that the customer is eligible for. This page helps your customers easily find and redeem all the available coupons in one place.

The Coupons page is tabbed with the Promotions page to let customers easily switch between these pages.

You can configure the following elements on the Coupons page:

  • Onboarding section
  • Coupon cards
  • Coupon details

Onboarding section

The Coupons page includes an onboarding section to guide your customers through the process of selecting and redeeming coupons, or provide any additional information.

Customers can choose to read through the content in the onboarding section or dismiss it.

You can customize the content in the onboarding section based on your requirements, using Lokalise.

Coupon cards

The Coupons page lists all the coupon promotions available to a customer as coupon cards.

You can display the following information on a coupon card:

Name - the name of the coupon promotion.

This is the value you set in:

  • displayAdditionalName, when using the Promotions API.
  • Coupon campaign title, when using the Promotions web app.

Discount value - the coupon promotion amount and a text string that you can define. For example, you can specify “Save £2.50” , “£2.50 off” , “Save 20%”, or “20% off”.

End date - the end date of the coupon. This is only displayed if you have set an end date for the coupon promotion. After a coupon promotion ends, the coupon card is no longer displayed on the Coupons page.

Product images - the images of the products that the coupon applies to.

The coupon card must include at least one product for it to be displayed on the Coupons page. You can display a maximum of 5 products on a coupon card.

When customers click a coupon card, they are redirected to the corresponding Coupon details page for that specific coupon promotion.

Coupon details

The Coupon details page lists all products associated with the specific coupon promotion.

You can display the following details on the Coupon details page:

Title - the title of the Coupon details page.

This is the value you set in:

  • displayName, when using the Promotions API.
  • Name, when using the Promotions web app.

Description - a description of the page.

This is the value you set in:

  • displayDescription, when using the Promotions API.
  • Description, when using the Promotions web app.

Product cards - the product cards of the different products associated with this coupon promotion.

Coupons on product cards and product details pages

After setting up a coupon promotion, you can have the coupon displayed on the product cards and product details pages of all eligible products.

You can display the following elements on the product card of an eligible product:

Coupon icon - a coupon icon to differentiate the coupon promotion from other promotion types. The coupon icon is also displayed on the product details page.

Coupon promotion name - the name of the coupon promotion.

This is the value you set in:

  • displayName, when using the Promotions API.
  • Name, when using the Promotions web app.

Reduced price - the price of the product after the coupon value is deducted.

Strikethrough original price - the original price of the product striked through to inform customers of the price reduction.

Set up a coupon promotion

You can set up and configure coupons in either of the following ways:

Using the Promotions web app

You can use the Promotions web app to set up and configure a coupon promotion. See the Promotions user guide for more information on how to create and set up a coupon promotion.

Setting up coupons using the Promotions web app is an optional functionality provided with the Coupons feature. Speak to your Ocado contact for more information.

Using the Promotions API

You can also use the Promotions API to create coupons. Coupons are set up as an item-level promotion. See Promotions API for more information on how to create coupons using this method.

Usage limits

You can define three different usage limits for coupons in your online store:

  • Uses per order - the number of times a coupon can be used in one order. For example, if this limit is set to 1, a customer will be able to use a coupon once in a given order but the coupon will be available for the next order again.

  • Global usage - the maximum number of times a coupon can be used during a coupon promotion campaign, for example, 5000.

  • Global usage per shopper - the maximum number of times a coupon can be used by a specific customer during a coupon promotion campaign, for example, 10 per customer.

Monitor coupon usage

You can monitor the global usage of a coupon promotion using the Promotions API. To get more detailed information about coupon usage, use the Orders data feed. You can identify the coupon promotions that were applied to an order using the coupon promotion IDs in the Orders data feed. This data feed lists all promotion IDs applied to a given order.

Customer journey

Your customers can interact with the coupons feature as follows:

Access the Coupons page

Depending on the platform your customers are operating from, they can access the Coupons page in the following ways:

  • Web version of your OSP online store - customers can click Coupons on the navigation bar to go to the Coupons page. The Coupons button also displays the number of coupons available to the customer.

    When using OSP web version of your online store on mobile devices, customers can access the navigation bar items using the hamburger menu.

  • Mobile app version of your OSP online store - customers can tap the Coupons tile under the Categories section to go to the Coupons page.

    Customers can also tap the Promotions tile to go to the Promotions menu, and then click the Coupons tab to go to the Coupons page.

    Customers can also get to the Promotions menu by clicking Browse on the home page and then clicking Promotions.

Redeem a coupon

Your customers can redeem a coupon through the following entry points:

Product cards and product details pages

When you set up a coupon in the Promotions web app or the Promotions API, the coupon is displayed on the product card and product details page of all eligible products.

Customers can redeem the coupon by adding the said product to their cart. The coupon value is automatically deducted from the product price.

Coupons page

Customers can go to the Coupons page that lists all the available coupon promotions as Coupon cards. Clicking a coupon card takes the customer to the respective Coupon details page that lists all the products associated with the selected coupon. Customers can add a product from this list and redeem the coupon linked to it.

Customers cannot redeem coupon discounts on subsequent eligible products added to cart after the Uses per order limit has been reached. However, currently OSP will still display the coupon promotion information on other eligible products.

No available coupons

When no coupons are available for the customer, the Coupons page displays a message with a Promotions button. Customers can click this button to go to the Promotions page and view existing promotions in your online store.
